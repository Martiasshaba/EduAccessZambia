Your gateway to educational opportunities and financial aid guidance.

About Us

EduAccess Zambia is dedicated to providing Zambian youth with access to scholarships, financial aid, and guidance to help them achieve their academic goals. We believe that by equipping students with the right resources, we can inspire them to excel in their studies and pursue higher education.

Our Programs

We offer a variety of programs aimed at supporting students in their educational journey, including scholarship guidance, study abroad expos, and application assistance.

Scholarships

  • Cavendish University Merit Scholarships ▼

    • Offered to school leavers with 6 to 10 points at Grade 12. Available for Zambian students.

  • Mutale Nalumango Foundation Scholarship ▼

    • Available for Zambian students at Cavendish University.

  • FQM Trident Scholarships ▼

    • Scholarships offered by First Quantum Minerals for outstanding Zambian students.

  • KCM Miners' Children A-Level Scholarship ▼

    • Scholarships for the children of miners provided by Konkola Copper Mines.

  • Chengelo School Scholarship ▼

    • Scholarships available for outstanding students at Chengelo School.

  • American International School of Lusaka Beacon Scholarship ▼

    • A prestigious scholarship for exceptional students at the American International School of Lusaka.

  • Baobab College Beacon Scholarship ▼

    • Scholarships for outstanding students at Baobab College.

  • United World Colleges (UWC) Scholarship ▼

    • Scholarships for the International Baccalaureate Diploma at UWC schools worldwide.

  • Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program ▼

    • Scholarships for studying at various universities including the University of Cape Town, University of Edinburgh, and others under the Mastercard Foundation.

  • Japanese MEXT Scholarship ▼

    • Scholarships provided by the Japanese government for undergraduate, graduate, and technical education in Japan.

  • Turkiye Burslari Scholarship ▼

    • Scholarships for studying in Turkey offered by the Turkish government.

Loans

  • HELSB Loans for Public Universities ▼

    • Student loans for Mulungushi University, Kwame Nkrumah University, Chalimbana University, CBU, UNZA, Mukuba University, and Palabana University provided by the Zambian government through the Higher Education Loans and Scholarships Board (HELSB).

  • HELSB Scholarships for Study Abroad ▼

    • Scholarships provided by HELSB for studying in countries such as Russia, India, Tunisia, Algeria, Egypt, Czech Republic, Ukraine, South Korea, China, Japan (MEXT), and Turkey (Turkiye Burslari).
